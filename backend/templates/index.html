The 4 Phases of Your Cycle

Understanding the hormonal fluctuations of your monthly journey is vital for overall wellness. Our AI uses these patterns to provide accuracy.

DAYS 1-5

Menstrual Phase

The uterus sheds its lining. Energy levels may be lower. Rest is encouraged.

DAYS 1-13

Follicular Phase

The body prepares to release an egg. Estrogen rises, boosting energy and focus.

DAY 14

Ovulation Phase

An egg is released. You are most fertile. Mood and libido often peak here.

DAYS 15-28

Luteal Phase

The body prepares for possible pregnancy. Progesterone rises; PMS may occur.
